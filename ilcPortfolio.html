About me

I come from a very large family. I grew up with people constantly around me, whether I wanted it or not. I am the only one with my Individuality and I want to use it to make an impact on the world.

I am determined to succeed, committed to doing what it takes, and passionate about creating a better life for myself and those around me.

I gained interest in technology and software engineering a few years ago from a roommate. I started working through an online bootcamp and really enjoyed it and learning there are so many possibilities you can apply this to. I also enjoy playing sports, outdoor activities and traveling .
